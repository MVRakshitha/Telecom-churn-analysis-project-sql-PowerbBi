# Telecom churn analysis -dashboard
## Recommended Structure and Order
### 1. Project Title / Headline
An interactive analytics project that analyzes customer behavior to identify key churn drivers and predict at-risk customers. It leverages data visualization and machine learning to generate actionable insights. The solution helps businesses improve customer retention and make data-driven decisions.

### 2. Short Description / Purpose
In today’s competitive business environment, retaining customers is crucial for long-term success. Churn analysis is a key technique used to understand and reduce this customer attrition. It involves examining customer data to identify patterns and reasons behind customer departures. By using advanced data analytics and machine learning, businesses can predict which customers are at risk of leaving and understand the factors driving their decisions. This knowledge allows companies to take proactive steps to improve customer satisfaction and loyalty.

### 3. Tech Stack
List the key technologies used to build the dashboard.

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
• 📂 Power Query – Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.
• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures, dynamic visuals, and conditional logic.
• 📝 Data Modeling – Relationships established among tables to enable cross-filtering and aggregation.
• 📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

### 4. Features / Highlights
The best dashboard explanation format. • Business problem • Goal of the dashboard • Walk through of key visuals (briefly!) • Business impact & Insights

• Business Problem Businesses are facing high customer churn, meaning customers are leaving their services, which directly impacts revenue and growth. Companies often lack visibility into why customers are leaving and which customers are at risk, making it difficult to take timely action.

• Project helps to:
Identify key reasons behind customer churn
Detect patterns in customer behavior
Predict which customers are likely to leave
Enable businesses to take proactive retention strategies

• Goal of the Dashboard
The goal of this dashboard is to analyze and reduce customer churn by providing insights into customer behavior, churn patterns, and key factors influencing customer attrition. It helps identify who is likely to churn, why they are leaving, and which segments are most affected.

Additionally, the dashboard supports data-driven decision-making by highlighting high-risk customers, churn trends across demographics and services, and key business drivers. This enables organizations to take proactive actions to improve customer retention, increase satisfaction, and minimize revenue loss.

Predicted Churn Profile (Left Panel) : Displays churn distribution by gender, age group, marital status, tenure, payment method, and contract type, helping identify high-risk customer segments.
Customers at Risk (Right Panel Table) : Shows a detailed list of predicted churners with revenue, referrals, and monthly charges, enabling targeted retention strategies.
Churn by Gender (Donut Chart) : Visualizes churn distribution between male and female customers to identify gender-based trends.
Churn Rate by Age Group (Bar Chart) : Highlights which age groups have higher churn rates, helping businesses focus on vulnerable segments.
Churn by State (Geographic Analysis) : Displays top states with highest churn rates, supporting region-based decision-making.
Churn by Internet Type (Bar Chart) : Compares churn across services like Fiber Optic, Cable, and DSL to identify service-related issues.
Churn by Payment Method (Bar Chart) : Shows how payment methods (Credit Card, Bank Withdrawal, Mailed Check) impact churn behavior.
Churn by Contract Type (Bar Chart) : Indicates that customers with month-to-month contracts have higher churn, while long-term contracts reduce churn.
Churn Distribution by Category (Bar Chart) : Breaks down churn reasons such as competitor offers, attitude, dissatisfaction, and pricing, highlighting key drivers.

• Business Impact & Insights

Customer Retention Strategy : Helps identify high-risk customers and take proactive actions to reduce churn.
Revenue Protection : By targeting customers likely to leave, businesses can minimize revenue loss.
Service Improvement : Insights into churn reasons (e.g., competitor offers, service quality) help improve offerings.
Targeted Marketing : Enables personalized campaigns based on demographics, location, and behavior.
Strategic Decision-Making : Supports data-driven decisions to optimize pricing, contracts, and customer experience.
